Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 7-day trial work?

You can use My Day Point fully for 7 days after sign-in. There is no credit card required for the trial. After day 7, score saving and group features are paused until you unlock lifetime access.

Is the purchase a subscription?

No. Lifetime access is a one-time payment of $2.99 USD (or local equivalent). There is no auto-renewal and nothing is billed again.

Can I restore my purchase on a new device?

Yes. Sign in with the same Apple ID you used for the purchase, open Profile and tap "Restore Purchases".

How do I delete my account?

Profile screen → "Delete account". This removes your scores, groups you own, and profile data within 30 days.

Can other people see my scores?

Only members of groups you create or join can see a score for a given day, and only after you submit a score that day. Otherwise everything is private.

Reminders aren't showing up.

iOS → Settings → Notifications → My Day Point → make sure "Allow Notifications" is on. Then in the App, open Profile and tap "Enable Reminder" again.

I forgot my password.

Email is.oztas@hotmail.com with the email you signed up with. We'll send a reset link.
